How to Use

Get your template in seconds.

01
Find the item on Roblox

Open the catalog and find the classic shirt or pants you want.

02
Copy the asset ID

Grab the number from the URL — roblox.com/catalog/123456789/...

03
Paste it into Clothify

Hit Download on the home page, paste the ID, and press Download template.

04
Preview and save

The template loads as a preview. Hit Save image to download the PNG.
